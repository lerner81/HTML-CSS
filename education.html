Jazz Education

Classes Offered

  1. The Art of Practicing - Practice does not make Perfect. Perfect Practice makes perfect. Learn how to practice correctly to maximize results. Mark breaks down how to spend your time when practicing. Master practice and master laser focus.

  2. Modern Trombone Technique Class - Empowering the future trombonists to play any style! This class focuses on slide techniques, flexibity, sound, articulations, and range.

  3. Plunger Technique - A lost art of plunger trombone. Learn the five plunger positions. The lost art of vocalizing the instrument. How to literally speak through the horn.

  4. Learn to Improvise - Learn how to start improvising. The concepts of building a solo. Focusing on the blues and the scales to use on all of the chords. How to approach a new solo and create a plan.

  5. Modern Improvisation - Take the knowledge of improvisation with you. This masterclass focuses on bebop scales, pentatonics, modal/chromaticism, and the magic exercise. A perfect class for more advanced improvisers.
